Kajam

Replit (not affiliated with BinSpaghetti, though it was, at one point, our primary IDE) hosts an annual game-jam called "KaJam". A portmanteau of "Kaboom" (a Replit game library for JavScript) and "jam" (as in game jam), the BinSpaghetti team will participate in the event starting in 2021.
